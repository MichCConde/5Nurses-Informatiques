WHAT IS HEALTH INFORMATICS?

Health informatics is a multidisciplinary field that leverages technology to collect, manage, and analyze health data, ultimately enhancing patient care and fostering the development of innovative healthcare solutions (Sutner & DelVecchio, 2018; Wired, 2024).
